Detailed Practice Areas

Click on any practice area below to learn more about how we can help.

We advise businesses of all sizes on company formation, mergers and acquisitions, shareholder agreements, commercial contracts, and regulatory compliance. Our corporate team has guided over 200 transactions, from small startups registering their first company to multinational mergers requiring complex due diligence.

From the moment of arrest through trial and appeal, our criminal defence advocates protect your constitutional rights. We handle cases ranging from minor offences to serious felonies, always with a focus on securing the fairest possible outcome for our clients.

Divorce, child custody, adoption, and matrimonial property disputes are handled with both legal precision and personal sensitivity. We understand these cases affect real families, and we work to find resolutions that protect our clients' long-term wellbeing.

We manage title searches, land transfers, lease agreements, and property disputes for both individuals and developers. Our team is well-versed in Kenya's land laws and works to ensure every transaction is secure and properly documented.

Work permits, residency applications, citizenship matters, and immigration appeals are handled efficiently by our dedicated immigration team, helping individuals and businesses navigate Kenya's immigration regulations with confidence.

We represent clients in contract disputes, tort claims, and other civil matters, pursuing resolution through both courtroom litigation and alternative dispute resolution where appropriate, always with a clear strategy aligned to the client's goals.
