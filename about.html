Our Story

Built on a single conviction

Blackwell & Associates was founded in 1998 by Advocate Daniel Blackwell, who began the firm with a single conviction: that every client, regardless of means, deserves a rigorous and honest defence of their rights.

What began as a two-person practice in a small Nairobi office has grown into one of the city's most respected full-service firms, now home to 18 senior advocates across six practice areas. Yet our founding principle remains unchanged — we represent people, not just cases.

Today, our clients range from individuals navigating family disputes to corporations managing complex regulatory matters, all served with the same level of diligence and care.
