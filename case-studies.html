Cross-Border Acquisition

Challenge: A Nairobi-based manufacturing firm needed to navigate a $4.2M acquisition offer from a regional competitor, involving cross-border regulatory approval and complex shareholder negotiations.

Approach: Our corporate team conducted full due diligence, restructured the shareholder agreement, and negotiated terms that protected minority shareholders.

Outcome: The acquisition closed successfully with terms 15% more favourable than the initial offer, and zero post-closing disputes.

Fraud Charges Dismissed

Challenge: Our client was charged with financial fraud based on circumstantial evidence from a former business partner.

Approach: Our criminal defence team built a detailed forensic accounting rebuttal and exposed inconsistencies in the prosecution's timeline.

Outcome: All charges were dismissed by the prosecution before the case reached trial.

Custody Resolution

Challenge: A high-conflict divorce with disputed custody of two minor children, complicated by relocation concerns.

Approach: Our family law team prioritized mediation, presenting a child-centered parenting plan supported by a court-appointed assessor's report.

Outcome: The court approved a joint custody arrangement that preserved stability for the children and was accepted by both parties.

Land Title Dispute

Challenge: A decade-long boundary dispute threatened a family's claim to ancestral land due to conflicting survey records.

Approach: Our property team commissioned an independent survey, cross-referenced historical land registry records, and represented the family through arbitration.

Outcome: Clear title was secured for the family, resolving the dispute permanently and without further litigation.
